Zion Temple Celebration Center

Zion Temple Celebration Center has outreach programs that include yearly conferences and seminars. The church also has a YouTube channel that records and uploads Sunday services.

Zion Temple churches worldwide

The Assemblies of World Mission/Zion Temple Christian Community (AWM/ZTCC) has churches in Africa, Europe, Asia, Australia, and North America.

Zion temple project

Zion Temple Arena

Zion Temple is planning to build an Arena Zion in Kigali, which will also utilize satellite technology.

This arena, located on Mount Hermon in the Giheka village, Kinyinya sector, Gasabo district, Kigali, is designed to accommodate 15,000 people and will operate 24/7.

The first phase of construction includes accommodation facilities and the Zion Arena itself.
